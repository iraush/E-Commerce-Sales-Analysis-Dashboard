# E-Commerce-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard

I have designed an interactive E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard in Power BI to monitor sales performance, profitability, order trends, and category-wise business insights. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key performance indicators through dynamic visualizations and filters, enabling data-driven decision-making and performance tracking.<br>

**Key Metrics**<br>
Sales: Displays the total revenue generated from customer orders.<br>
Profit: Shows the overall profit earned during the selected period.<br>
Quantity: Represents the total number of products sold.<br>
No. of Orders: Displays the total number of customer orders placed.<br>
Profit Margin: Measures profitability as a percentage of total sales.<br>

**Interactive Filters**<br>
Year Filter: Allows users to analyze data for specific years.<br>
Region Filter: Enables regional performance comparison.<br>
Segment Filter: Provides insights across Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office customer segments.<br>

**Visualizations Used**<br>

Sales and Profit Analysis :<br>
Combination chart displaying monthly sales and profit trends.<br>
Helps identify seasonal patterns and business performance fluctuations.<br>

Category Wise Profit Analysis:<br>
Waterfall chart showing profit contribution by product category.<br>
Highlights categories that contribute most to overall profitability.<br>

Category Wise Sales Share:<br>
Donut chart representing the percentage contribution of each category to total sales.<br>
Provides a quick overview of category performance.<br>

Sales by State:<br>
Geographic map visualizing sales distribution across states.<br>
Helps identify high-performing and low-performing regions.<br>

Top 10 Sub-Categories:<br>
Bar chart displaying the best-performing product sub-categories based on sales.<br>
Enables quick identification of top revenue-generating products.<br>

**Feature**s<br>
Dynamic KPI cards with Year-over-Year (YoY) growth indicators.<br>
Interactive slicers for customized analysis.<br>
Geographic sales analysis using map visualization.<br>
Category and sub-category performance tracking.<br>
Profitability and sales trend monitoring.<br>
Professional dark-themed dashboard design.<br>

**Business Value**<br>
This dashboard helps businesses track revenue growth, monitor profitability, analyze customer segments, evaluate regional performance, and identify top-performing product categories. It supports strategic decision-making by providing actionable insights into sales and operational performance.<br>
